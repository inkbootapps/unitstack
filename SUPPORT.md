## Common questions

### Where are my conversions stored?
UnitStack stores your saved conversions locally on your device (and/or iCloud if you enabled it, if applicable).

### How do I restore purchases?
UnitStack has no in-app purchases. In general try below for restoring purchases:
(If you have IAP) Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchases.

### I found a bug
Please email or open a GitHub issue with steps to reproduce.
